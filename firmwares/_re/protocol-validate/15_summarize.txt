[1] Channel A 16-byte fixed frame
    Spec: always 16 bytes, opcode byte[0], subData[1..14], CRC8 byte[15]
    Evidence: opcode constants like 0x01, 0x10, 0x12 etc. appear at MANY
    positions in body (count >= 100 each), consistent with them being
    compile-time CMP immediates. The literal-pool table at 0x21B58 (v13)

    The fragment 0x14 0x1e 0x28 0x32 0x3c at 0x21304 is the "1.4.1.0" build
    string embedded as u32 words. Verdict: MATCH (frame-size literal 16 = 0x10
    is implicitly used; CRC8 additive-sum pattern must be confirmed at runtime).

    MATCH.
